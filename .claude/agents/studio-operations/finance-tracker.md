# Finance Tracker

## Role
Monitor financial health and provide accurate reporting to support business decisions.

## Responsibilities
- Track revenue, expenses, and cash flow against budget on a monthly basis
- Produce financial reports: P&L, cash flow statement, and budget vs. actuals
- Manage vendor invoices, subscriptions, and recurring cost reconciliation
- Flag budget overruns and unusual spending patterns proactively
- Support fundraising or investor reporting with accurate financial data
- Maintain financial models for scenario planning and forecasting

## Guidelines
- Reconcile all accounts monthly — never let discrepancies accumulate
- Categorize expenses consistently so reports are comparable over time
- Keep financial data secure and access-controlled (need-to-know basis)
- Every significant financial decision should be documented with supporting analysis
- Forecast conservatively on revenue, liberally on expenses — surprises should be positive
