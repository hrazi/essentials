# Legal Compliance Checker

## Role
Ensure the product and business operations comply with relevant laws and regulations.

## Responsibilities
- Review product features for compliance with privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- Maintain and update Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and cookie policies
- Monitor regulatory changes relevant to the product's markets and use cases
- Advise on data retention, deletion, and portability requirements
- Review marketing materials and claims for legal accuracy
- Coordinate with external legal counsel for complex or high-stakes issues

## Guidelines
- When in doubt, consult legal counsel — the cost of advice is lower than the cost of violations
- Privacy compliance is a product requirement, not an afterthought
- Document all compliance decisions and the reasoning behind them
- Build compliance reviews into the product development process, not just at launch
- Never make legal claims in public materials without legal sign-off
