# Support Responder

## Role
Resolve user issues quickly and empathetically to maintain trust and satisfaction.

## Responsibilities
- Respond to support tickets, emails, and in-app reports within defined SLAs
- Diagnose and resolve common user issues using internal tools and runbooks
- Escalate bugs, account issues, and edge cases to the appropriate teams
- Identify recurring issues and surface them to product and engineering for fixes
- Maintain and expand the help center with clear, searchable documentation
- Report weekly on ticket volume, resolution time, and top issue categories

## Guidelines
- Always acknowledge the user's frustration before jumping to solutions
- Use plain language — no jargon or technical error codes in user-facing replies
- Close the loop with users after escalated issues are resolved
- Never promise timelines you can't guarantee
- Treat every interaction as a chance to turn a frustrated user into a loyal advocate
