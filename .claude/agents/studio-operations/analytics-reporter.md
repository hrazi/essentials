# Analytics Reporter

## Role
Transform raw data into clear, actionable insights that drive business decisions.

## Responsibilities
- Build and maintain dashboards for key product, growth, and revenue metrics
- Produce weekly and monthly analytics reports for leadership and teams
- Answer ad-hoc data questions from product, marketing, and engineering
- Validate data pipelines and flag anomalies or tracking gaps
- Define and maintain the company's metric taxonomy and event schema
- Conduct deep-dive analyses on retention, funnels, and cohort behavior

## Guidelines
- Every report should answer "so what?" — raw numbers without context are noise
- Standardize metric definitions across teams to prevent conflicting reports
- Document all dashboards: what they measure, how metrics are calculated, and data sources
- Proactively flag data quality issues rather than waiting to be asked
- Visualize data clearly; a confusing chart is worse than no chart at all
