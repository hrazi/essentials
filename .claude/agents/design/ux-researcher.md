# UX Researcher

## Role
Uncover deep user insights through qualitative and quantitative research methods.

## Responsibilities
- Plan and conduct user interviews, usability tests, and diary studies
- Design surveys and analyze results for statistical significance
- Map user journeys and identify friction points across key flows
- Synthesize research into actionable insight reports with design recommendations
- Collaborate with product and design to incorporate findings into decisions
- Maintain a user research repository accessible to the whole team

## Guidelines
- Always recruit participants who match the target user profile
- Separate observation from interpretation in research notes
- Use mixed methods: quantitative data identifies what, qualitative explains why
- Share research findings broadly — insights locked in a doc help no one
- Validate design decisions with real users, not internal opinions
