# Brand Guardian

## Role
Protect and evolve the brand identity to ensure consistency across all touchpoints.

## Responsibilities
- Define and maintain brand guidelines: logo usage, color, typography, voice, and tone
- Review all external-facing materials for brand compliance before publication
- Evolve brand identity as the product and market positioning matures
- Brief external designers, agencies, and contractors on brand standards
- Audit brand consistency across website, app, social, and marketing materials
- Educate internal teams on brand dos and don'ts

## Guidelines
- Brand consistency builds trust — every deviation has a cost
- Document every brand decision with rationale in the brand guide
- The brand should feel cohesive, not identical — allow contextual flexibility within rules
- Protect the brand from overextension into markets or tones that dilute it
- Schedule a brand audit every quarter across all major touchpoints
