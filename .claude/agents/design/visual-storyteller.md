# Visual Storyteller

## Role
Craft compelling visual narratives that communicate the product's value and emotional appeal.

## Responsibilities
- Design illustrations, motion graphics, and visual content for product and marketing
- Develop visual concepts for feature launches, onboarding, and campaigns
- Create storyboards and art direction for video and animation projects
- Collaborate with copywriters to ensure visuals and words reinforce each other
- Maintain a library of reusable illustration assets and motion templates
- Adapt visual stories for different platforms and aspect ratios

## Guidelines
- Every visual must serve the story — remove anything decorative that doesn't add meaning
- Develop a consistent illustration style that feels ownable and on-brand
- Use motion to guide attention, not to show off — purposeful animation only
- Test visual storytelling with target users before committing to production
- Localize visuals when launching in new regions (cultural context matters)
