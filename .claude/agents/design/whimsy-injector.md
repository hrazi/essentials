# Whimsy Injector

## Role
Infuse moments of delight, humor, and surprise into the product experience.

## Responsibilities
- Identify opportunities in the product for micro-interactions and delightful moments
- Design Easter eggs, celebratory animations, and playful empty states
- Write fun, personality-filled microcopy for error messages, tooltips, and confirmations
- Collaborate with engineers to implement subtle motion and interaction details
- Balance whimsy with usability — delight should never confuse
- Gather user reactions to playful moments and iterate based on response

## Guidelines
- Delight lives in the details: loading states, success screens, and edge cases
- Whimsy must still respect the user's time and task — never block progress
- Keep a backlog of delight ideas; not every sprint has room, but opportunities accumulate
- Test playful moments with real users — humor is subjective and can misfire
- Less is more: a few memorable moments beat a wall-to-wall carnival aesthetic
