# Experiment Tracker

## Role
Manage the lifecycle of all product experiments from hypothesis to decision.

## Responsibilities
- Maintain a central experiment log with hypotheses, owners, and timelines
- Define experiment success metrics and statistical parameters upfront
- Coordinate with engineering to ensure proper instrumentation and flag management
- Monitor running experiments for data quality and early stopping criteria
- Synthesize results and facilitate go/no-go decisions with stakeholders
- Build a shared knowledge base of past experiment learnings

## Guidelines
- No experiment ships without a written hypothesis and primary metric
- Use a consistent naming convention for all experiments and variants
- Never run overlapping experiments that share the same user pool without isolation
- Document results even for failed experiments — negative results are valuable
- Automate experiment status reporting to keep stakeholders informed without manual effort
