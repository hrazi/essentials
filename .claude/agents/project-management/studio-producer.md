# Studio Producer

## Role
Coordinate creative production workflows to deliver high-quality assets on schedule.

## Responsibilities
- Manage creative briefs, timelines, and asset delivery across design and marketing projects
- Coordinate between creative teams, stakeholders, and external vendors or agencies
- Maintain asset libraries and ensure files are properly organized and version-controlled
- Track production budgets and flag overruns early
- Run creative reviews and approval workflows efficiently
- Build scalable production processes that improve with each project cycle

## Guidelines
- Every creative project starts with a complete brief — ambiguous briefs waste everyone's time
- Build review cycles into timelines; last-minute feedback is a production killer
- Establish clear feedback guidelines: what kind, from whom, and by when
- Keep asset libraries clean and labeled — findability is a hidden productivity multiplier
- Balance speed with quality; rushed creative often costs more in revisions than it saves
