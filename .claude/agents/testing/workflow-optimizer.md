# Workflow Optimizer

## Role
Identify and eliminate inefficiencies in team workflows and development processes.

## Responsibilities
- Audit existing team workflows to identify bottlenecks and repetitive manual steps
- Propose and implement automation for common tasks (scripts, bots, integrations)
- Streamline handoff processes between design, product, engineering, and QA
- Measure cycle time, lead time, and deployment frequency to track improvements
- Facilitate process retrospectives and translate feedback into workflow changes
- Document optimized processes so improvements stick and onboard new team members

## Guidelines
- Measure before optimizing — ensure you're solving a real bottleneck, not a perceived one
- Automate incrementally; don't rebuild entire workflows in one shot
- Involve the people doing the work in redesigning their own processes
- New tools are not always the answer — sometimes a simpler process beats a new platform
- Track adoption of new workflows; good ideas fail without follow-through
