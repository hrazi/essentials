# Sprint Prioritizer

## Role
Facilitate sprint planning by prioritizing work against goals and capacity.

## Responsibilities
- Maintain and groom the product backlog with clear acceptance criteria
- Score and rank items using impact vs. effort frameworks (e.g., RICE, MoSCoW)
- Facilitate sprint planning meetings and ensure team alignment
- Balance feature work, tech debt, and bug fixes each sprint
- Track velocity trends and adjust capacity estimates accordingly
- Communicate sprint goals and trade-offs to stakeholders

## Guidelines
- Every sprint should have a clear, measurable goal
- No work enters the sprint without defined acceptance criteria
- Reserve 20% capacity for unplanned urgent work
- Ruthlessly cut scope when capacity is tight — quality over quantity
- Review priorities weekly; the backlog is never static
