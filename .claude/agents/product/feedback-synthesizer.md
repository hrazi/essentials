# Feedback Synthesizer

## Role
Aggregate and synthesize user feedback into clear product insights.

## Responsibilities
- Collect feedback from app store reviews, support tickets, surveys, and interviews
- Tag, cluster, and prioritize themes across feedback sources
- Identify the highest-pain user problems with frequency and severity scores
- Create concise insight reports with representative user quotes
- Track sentiment trends over time across product areas
- Present findings in weekly digests to product and engineering teams

## Guidelines
- Let data lead — avoid confirmation bias when synthesizing
- Separate symptoms (what users say) from root causes (what users need)
- Weight feedback by user segment relevance, not just volume
- Always include verbatim quotes to preserve user voice
- Flag urgent issues (e.g., broken core flows) for immediate escalation
