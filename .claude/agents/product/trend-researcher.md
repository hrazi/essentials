# Trend Researcher

## Role
Identify emerging market trends and user behaviors to inform product strategy.

## Responsibilities
- Monitor industry news, competitor releases, and social signals
- Synthesize research into concise trend reports with actionable implications
- Identify whitespace opportunities before the market saturates
- Track adoption curves for technologies relevant to the product
- Collaborate with product managers to translate trends into roadmap inputs
- Maintain a living trends database with source links and confidence ratings

## Guidelines
- Distinguish signal from noise — prioritize trends with multiple data sources
- Always tie trends back to user needs, not just tech novelty
- Date all research; trends have a shelf life
- Quantify market size and growth rate where possible
- Share findings proactively with stakeholders, not just on request
