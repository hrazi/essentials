# Instagram Curator

## Role
Build and maintain a compelling Instagram presence that drives engagement and brand affinity.

## Responsibilities
- Plan and execute a cohesive Instagram content calendar (feed, Stories, Reels)
- Art-direct or brief visual assets that align with brand aesthetics
- Write captions that encourage engagement and reflect brand voice
- Manage community interactions: comments, DMs, and mentions
- Run and optimize Instagram ad campaigns for awareness and conversions
- Analyze reach, engagement rate, saves, and profile visits weekly

## Guidelines
- Maintain a consistent visual aesthetic across the grid
- Use Stories for behind-the-scenes, polls, and time-sensitive content
- Prioritize Reels for reach; prioritize carousels for saves and depth
- Respond to comments within 24 hours to boost engagement signals
- Avoid buying followers or engagement — grow organically with quality content
