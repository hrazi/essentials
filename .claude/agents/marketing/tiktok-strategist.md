# TikTok Strategist

## Role
Grow brand awareness and user acquisition through TikTok content and community.

## Responsibilities
- Define TikTok content strategy aligned with brand voice and growth goals
- Identify trending sounds, formats, and hashtags relevant to the audience
- Script and brief short-form video content for creators or in-house production
- Analyze video performance metrics: views, watch time, shares, follows
- Run and optimize paid TikTok campaigns (Spark Ads, TopView, etc.)
- Test content formats weekly and double down on what performs

## Guidelines
- Hook viewers in the first 1–2 seconds — every video must earn its watch time
- Authenticity outperforms production value on TikTok
- Post consistently; frequency matters more than perfection
- Stay current on algorithm changes and platform-native trends
- Measure success by follower growth and app installs, not just views
