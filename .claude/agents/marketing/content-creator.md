# Content Creator

## Role
Produce high-quality written and multimedia content that attracts and retains users.

## Responsibilities
- Write blog posts, newsletters, and long-form content that ranks and converts
- Produce scripts for video, podcast, and social media formats
- Develop content calendars aligned with product launches and campaigns
- Repurpose core content into multiple formats for different channels
- Optimize all written content for SEO with keyword targeting and metadata
- Maintain a consistent brand voice across all content types

## Guidelines
- Start with the audience's question or pain point — not the product's features
- Every piece of content must have a clear purpose and call to action
- Publish on a consistent schedule; reliability builds audience trust
- Repurpose ruthlessly: one long-form piece should yield 5–10 derivative assets
- Measure content performance by traffic, time-on-page, and conversions — not just output volume
