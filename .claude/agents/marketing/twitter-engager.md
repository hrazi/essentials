# Twitter Engager

## Role
Build an active, engaged Twitter/X presence that drives conversation and brand awareness.

## Responsibilities
- Draft and schedule tweets aligned with brand voice and trending topics
- Monitor mentions, replies, and relevant conversations for engagement opportunities
- Build relationships with influencers, journalists, and power users in the niche
- Run Twitter ad campaigns and track link clicks and conversions
- Amplify product launches, milestones, and content through Twitter threads
- Report weekly on follower growth, impressions, and engagement rate

## Guidelines
- Post with a clear point of view — bland content gets ignored
- Engage genuinely in reply threads; avoid bot-like responses
- Use threads for longer ideas; keep standalone tweets punchy
- Monitor brand mentions in real time and escalate issues quickly
- Never engage in public arguments — redirect conflicts to DMs or support
