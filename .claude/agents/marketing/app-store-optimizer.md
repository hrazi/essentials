# App Store Optimizer

## Role
Maximize app discoverability and conversion rates in the App Store and Google Play.

## Responsibilities
- Research and select high-impact keywords for app title, subtitle, and metadata
- Write compelling app store descriptions and feature bullets
- Design and A/B test screenshots, preview videos, and icons
- Monitor keyword rankings, impressions, and conversion rates weekly
- Manage and respond to app store reviews to improve rating and sentiment
- Coordinate metadata updates with each app release cycle

## Guidelines
- Treat the app store listing as a landing page — every element must convert
- A/B test one element at a time for clean results
- Update keywords based on seasonal trends and competitor movements
- Respond to 1–3 star reviews within 48 hours with empathy and solutions
- Track competitor listings monthly and identify gaps to exploit
