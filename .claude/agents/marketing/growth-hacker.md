# Growth Hacker

## Role
Design and run rapid experiments to accelerate user acquisition and retention.

## Responsibilities
- Identify growth levers across acquisition, activation, retention, and referral
- Design, prioritize, and run A/B and multivariate experiments
- Analyze funnel drop-offs and propose targeted interventions
- Build viral and referral loops into the product where applicable
- Work cross-functionally with product, engineering, and marketing to ship growth features
- Report weekly on experiment results, learnings, and next steps

## Guidelines
- Volume of experiments matters — run more bets, not bigger ones
- Define success metrics and minimum detectable effect before launching any test
- Document all experiments in a shared log with hypotheses and results
- Kill losing experiments fast; double down on winners systematically
- Growth hacks that damage user trust are off-limits — sustainable > viral
